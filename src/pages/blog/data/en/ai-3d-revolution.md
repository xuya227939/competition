## Challenges of Traditional 3D Modeling

Traditional 3D modeling processes typically require:

- Hours or even days of modeling time
- Professional 3D modeling software skills
- Complex material and texture setup
- Extensive manual adjustments and optimization
- High learning costs and time investment

Users only need to describe the 3D model they want to create in natural language, and Meshivo 3D's AI system can understand and parse these descriptions, automatically generating corresponding 3D geometry.

### 2. Automatic Material Matching

The AI system can intelligently analyze model features and automatically select the most suitable materials and textures, greatly reducing the need for manual adjustments.

Meshivo 3D provides one-click optimization functionality that can automatically fix problems in models and improve overall quality.
